# Delivery Ops Quick Brief

## Context
Regional logistics team scaling next-day deliveries across 3 cities. Manual routing causes delays and inconsistent ETAs.

## Goal
Automate dispatch, route optimization, and status updates to reduce late deliveries by 30%.

## Constraints
Legacy ERP integration, limited driver smartphones, peak-hour congestion.

## Target users
Dispatchers, drivers, customer support.

## Success metrics
On-time rate, average route time, support tickets per delivery.
