# Ecommerce Growth Quick Brief

## Context
DTC brand with seasonal spikes and repeat customers. Cart abandonment high on mobile, limited personalization.

## Goal
Improve conversion with better PDP content, streamlined checkout, and lifecycle emails.

## Constraints
Legacy CMS for PDP, payment provider limits, small growth team.

## Target users
New shoppers, returning customers, marketing operations.

## Success metrics
Add-to-cart rate, checkout completion, repeat purchase rate, revenue per session.
