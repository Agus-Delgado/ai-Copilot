# SaaS B2B Quick Brief

## Context
B2B analytics product adding workspace sharing and approvals. Customers need clearer roles and audit history.

## Goal
Enable collaborative workflows with role-based access, approvals, and activity logs.

## Constraints
Multi-tenant architecture, enterprise SSO, limited backend capacity.

## Target users
Workspace admins, analysts, viewers, auditors.

## Success metrics
Active collaborations per workspace, approval turnaround, support escalations.
