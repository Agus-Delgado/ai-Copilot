# Healthcare Clinic Quick Brief

## Context
Multi-specialty clinic expanding online appointments and remote follow-ups. Paper intake forms slow down visits and data entry.

## Goal
Digitize intake, scheduling, and follow-up reminders to reduce no-shows and admin time.

## Constraints
HIPAA compliance, limited staff training time, legacy EHR integration.

## Target users
Reception, nurses, physicians, patients.

## Success metrics
No-show rate, average intake time, patient satisfaction (CSAT), staff hours saved.
